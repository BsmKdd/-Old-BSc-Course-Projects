The best part of your day - everyday!

We promise to be the best part of your day - everyday!

Whether you want to drop a few kilos, feel better and more energetic, improve your performance or prepare to compete for a national or world championship, StrengthHQ can help turn your dreams into a reality.

Our vision is to help motivate, guide and inspire you by providing a gym that encompasses: camaraderie, team work, work ethic, commitment to purpose, achievement and a positive, supportive atmosphere.

Every inch of our 800+ square metres is designed to work for YOU; from functional battle ropes, prowlers, sleds, tyres to state of the art Hammer Strength equipment, Bootcamps, Powerlifting and Olympic lifting platforms including a fully equipped Pilates studio.

  • Extensive free weight area
  • Olympic Weightlifting platforms and equipment
  • Powerlifting platforms and equipment
  • Four power racks
  • Dumbbells up to 50kg
  • Kettlebells
  • Strongman training equipment
  • Large group training area
  • Plyometrics equipment
  • Speed and agility equipment
  • Comprehensive cardiovascular equipment
  • Stretching and core training area
  • Bathroom, shower and changing facilities
